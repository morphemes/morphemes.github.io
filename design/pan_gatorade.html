G Fit Station Generator and Ad Campaign
I worked on a campaign for Gatorade's G Fit products. This was a series of Pandora playlists based on votes from Pandora users. I designed the display ads, mobile and desktop forms, and html email. Once the stations were compiled, I built a drag and drop station player inspired by various workouts.
Role - interactive design
Client - Gatorade
